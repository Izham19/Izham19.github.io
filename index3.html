MY EXPERIENCE

My first job as a salesman.
I have worked as a salesman for my cousin. He's selling a car perfume called 'Fluer'. The perfume got many variety of flavour like apple, honeydew and orange. The best seller is Honeydew flavour. I do this job for about 3 months from 20 July 2020 until 23 October 2020. It is a good experience for me to learning about engaging customers and sell the products. It gave enormous satisfaction to gain something news in my life.
My second job.
I had working at 7evelen about 2 months from 15 November 2020 until 25 January 2021. The unsual experience that I got from working at 7evelen is to have morning and night shift. I usually like to worked at night shift because there are less customers but but have to refrain myself from being sleepy. The advantage working on morning shift is i got to spend more time with my family but i will feel more tired because there are many works to do on morning shift like arrange the goods on the shelves. However, i had not worked long at there because too much energy required when working at convenience store that opens 24 hours.
My third job as a waiter at Seafood restaurant.
After finished my SPM exam, i worked at restaurant named seafood restaurant. My the period I worked there was for 2 months only because i had to do many things, like taking food orders, washing the dining table and washing dishes. It took a lot of my energy because the operating hour start at 5p.m. until 2a.m. where i washing the dishes before i went home. From that moment, i began to understand that finding the money was not an easy thing. I feel very grateful for what I have gained in my life.
My fourth job as a driver.
Start from february 2021 until now, i become a driver for my sister and grandma. They usually sell cakes and dishes, their customers will order first and we will send it the next day. The cakes that are usually sold are rokok arab and qasidah meanwhile dishes are 'kerutuk ayam' and 'kurma ayam' and 'kuzi ayam'. The surplus side dishes will be sold at the market known as 'Pasar Payang'. My sister and grandma will also take bookings at large quantities from customers. They tend to order more due to the big occasion like wedding or birthday party.
